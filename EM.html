Sarawak Cultural Heritage

Traditional Clothing

Batik is a traditional textile art form characterized by the application of wax to fabric, creating patterns that resist dye during the dyeing process.

Price starting from RM100.00 onwards.

Traditional Food

Savor Sarawak's unique flavors including the famous Laksa Sarawak, Manok Pansoh (bamboo chicken), and Umai (raw fish salad). Each dish tells a story of the state's diverse ethnic communities.

Cooking classes from RM80.00 per session.

Traditional Games

Experience indigenous games like Sumpit (blowpipe hunting), Batu Seremban (stone tossing), and Congkak that have been part of Sarawakian culture for generations in longhouse communities.

Workshops available from RM50.00 per person.

Traditional Culture

Immerse in Sarawak's living traditions through Iban longhouse stays, Orang Ulu woodcarving, Kenyah sape music, and Bidayuh bamboo crafts that showcase the state's multicultural heritage.

Cultural tours from RM120.00 per person.

Traditional Festival

Celebrate vibrant festivals like Gawai Dayak (harvest festival), Rainforest World Music Festival, and Pesta Kaul that highlight Sarawak's indigenous traditions through music, dance and rituals.

Festival packages from RM200.00.
