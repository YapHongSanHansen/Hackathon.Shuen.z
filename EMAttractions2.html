Baju Kurung Sarawak

The classic Sarawak Malay attire featuring intricate songket weaving with gold or silver threads. Typically worn with a matching kain sarung and tudung.

Ngepan Iban

Traditional Iban costume consisting of a maroon or black velvet jacket with silver ornaments for men, and a hand-woven skirt with silver belts for women.
