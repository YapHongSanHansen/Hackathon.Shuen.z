Traditional Clothing

Kelantan is renowned for its colorful Batik and Songket. The traditional Baju Melayu and Baju Kurung are worn with pride during cultural ceremonies and religious events.

Traditional Games

Kelantan is famous for Wau Bulan (giant moon kite). Locals also enjoy games like Sepak Raga and Congkak, which are often showcased at cultural festivals.

Traditional Culture

Kelantan is the heart of Malay traditional arts, including Wayang Kulit (shadow puppets), Mak Yong (dance-drama), and intricate wood carving.

Traditional Festivals

Celebrate rich traditions through festivals like Hari Raya Aidilfitri, Maulidur Rasul, and Islamic Arts celebrations, reflecting Kelantan's deep spiritual roots.
