Sabah Cultural Heritage

Traditional Clothing

Explore Sabah's traditional attire, such as the Kadazan-Dusun dress with its black velvet and gold trimmings, and the colorful Bajau costumes adorned with beads and embroidery.

Authentic pieces available from RM150.00 onwards.

Traditional Food

Enjoy Sabah’s specialties like Hinava (raw fish salad), Bambangan (fermented fruit), and Ambuyat. These local dishes are a culinary adventure rooted in tradition and flavor.

Cooking classes from RM80.00 per session.

Traditional Games

Learn and play games like Sumazau dance competitions, blowpipe contests, and Slippery Pole challenges often seen during local festivals and community gatherings in Sabah.

Workshops available from RM50.00 per person.

Traditional Culture

Immerse in Sabah's cultural richness through Murut longhouse stays, Kadazan-Dusun rituals, Bajau horse-riding culture, and Rungus handicrafts passed down through generations.

Cultural tours from RM120.00 per person.

Traditional Festival

Celebrate Sabah’s major festivals like Kaamatan (Harvest Festival), Regatta Lepa, and Tamu Besar that showcase native dance, food, and vibrant cultural performances.

Festival packages from RM200.00.
